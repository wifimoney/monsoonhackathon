# Monsoon: 1-Minute Demo Script

**Host**: "Hi, we are Monsoon. We're building the first **Agentic Asset Manager** that combines the flexibility of Valantis ALMs with the safety of Salt Guardrails."

---

### Scene 1: The Intelligent Vault (0:00 - 0:20)
**Visual**: User is on the **Vault Page**.
**Action**:
1.  Show the **"Yield Bucket"** statistic.
2.  **Voiceover**: "Our Sovereign ALM is unique. It doesn't just sit there. It dynamically routes idle liquidity to yield sources like Aave or Chorus One when not in use."
3.  **Visual**: Highlight the **"Dynamic Spread"**.
4.  **Voiceover**: "And it pricing is intelligent—using our Dynamic Module to adjust spreads based on real-time inventory skew."

### Scene 2: The Guarded Agent (0:20 - 0:45)
**Visual**: User switches to **Trade (Agent) Tab**.
**Action**:
1.  Type: **"Buy $100 ETH"**.
2.  Click **Execute**. It succeeds instantly via Pear Protocol.
3.  **Voiceover**: "But here's the magic. Users can trade with natural language."
4.  Type: **"Buy $500 ETH"** (This is above the $250 limit).
5.  Click **Execute**.
6.  **Visual**: **RED TOAST NOTIFICATION appears: "Policy Violation: Max Per Trade exceeded".**
7.  **Voiceover**: "But they can't go rogue. Our **Salt Guardrails** intercept the AI's intent and BLOCK the trade instantly before it ever touches the chain."

### Scene 3: The Guardian Dashboard (0:45 - 1:00)
**Visual**: User switches to **Guardians Tab**.
**Action**:
1.  Click **"Configure"** on the Leverage Guardian.
2.  Adjust the slider from **1x** to **50x**.
3.  **Voiceover**: "Admins have full control. They can configure these policies in real-time using our Guardian Dashboard—setting leverage limits, spend caps, and approved venues."
4.  **Closing**: "Monsoon: Safe, Intelligent, Sovereign Liquidity."

---

### Setup for Demo
1.  Ensure you are logged in (or have the session ready).
2.  Have the **Guardians** page configured with "Max Spend: $250".
3.  Pre-load the **Trade** page to avoid loading spinners.
